How does Copper work?

  • 1

    Check when the next event starts

    Check our events page for details of when the next Copper event is, and what time it starts. All events take place on this website.

  • 2

    Enter your information and join the event

    When the event starts, a button will appear on this page. Click the button, enter your details and join the event.

  • 3

    Video date other people near you

    Enjoy short videos dates with people near you. After each date, you can like that person. If you both like each other, it's a match.

  • 4

    Exchange contact details with your matches

    The day after the event, your contact details will be emailed to any matches you may have, so that you can continue messaging each other.
